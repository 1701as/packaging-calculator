5 Steps to LUCID Compliance

A simplified guide for Shopify merchants shipping to Germany.

1

Register with the ZSVR (LUCID)

You must register as a "Manufacturer" (even if you just pack boxes) in the official government database.

Go to LUCID Registration →
2

Buy a License (Dual System)

You cannot pay the government directly. You must contract a "Dual System" partner to recycle your waste. Popular providers include:

Report your Data (The Forecast)

You need to estimate how many kilograms of Paper and Plastic you will ship to Germany in the coming year. This is where Tare.fyi helps.

"Don't guess. Under-reporting can lead to audits. Use our calculator to get precise per-box weights."

4

Enter Data into LUCID

Take the volume report from your Dual System provider and enter the exact same numbers into your LUCID dashboard under "Data Report".

5

Repeat Annually

File your "Year-End Report" (Actuals) and next year's "Forecast" every December.
